About the Project

Project by: Jaxon Singleton & Ethan Wenthe

Use Case: We desired to design an application were users are able to seek out topic and representitives currently in congress to better their understanding of who they are voting for.

Structure: To achieve this the application pulls congressional bills from the House and the Senate using Congress.api and enacted a bm25 algorithm to return relevant bills based on the topic asked. The returns will also reveil the votes of representitives, to better fit the use case of this application.
