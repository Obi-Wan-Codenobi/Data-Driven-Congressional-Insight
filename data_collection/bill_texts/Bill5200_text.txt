R. 5200IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESAugust 11, 2023Mr. Steube introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the JudiciaryA BILLTo require a report from the Director of the Secret Service on illicit use of controlled substances at the Executive Residence. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Helping Understand Narcotics Traces at the Executive Residence Act of 2023 or as the HUNTER Act of 2023. 2.Report on illicit use of controlled substances in the White HouseNot later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Director of the Secret Service shall submit a report to Congress on the illicit use of controlled substances present at the White House, which shall includeâ€” 
(1)steps taken to avoid controlled substances from coming into the White House; (2)vulnerabilities where drugs can make its way into the White House; 
(3)a full account of the investigation into the cocaine found at the White House in 2023; and (4)recommendations on how Congress can provide oversight or resources to prevent illicit usage of controlled substances in the Executive Branch.
